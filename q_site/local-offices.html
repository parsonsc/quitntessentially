QUINTESSENTIALLY MEMBERS HAVE ACCES TO THOUSAND OF BENEFITS

OKKU

One of the best things about this smart restaurant-bar is that you can enjoy a whole night out in one location. Sip delicious mixed drinks at the bar before gorging on top-notch sushi, then mingle with like-minded souls to the sounds of a live DJ on Thursdays. This place is certainly a crowd-pleaser. Okku definitely stands out among all the trendy Japanese restaurants here in Dubai.

In terms of food, ambience, presentation and delivery. It has an upbeat lounge-like feel, so whether you’re up for a big night out or just dinner, you get the best of both worlds. The superlative service and chic presentation add to making Okku a force to be reckoned with.

DESERT PALM DUBAI

Half an hour’s drive from Downtown Dubai and 20 minutes from the airport, Desert Palms offers its guests an opportunity to escape the metropolis without having to venture into the desert. The hotel sits right on one of four palm-fringed polo fields and you can watch chukkas from the terrace-lounge, your bedroom window or even the gym called, appropriately, The Stables.

Spanish hacienda meets country club, a giant statue of a horse’s head in the outer courtyard sets the tone: this is a hotel obsessed with all things equine. In the basement is the luxurious Lime spa. Authentic and luxurious in equal measure.
