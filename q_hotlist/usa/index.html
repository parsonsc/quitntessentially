Sundance Film Festival 2018

When Thursday, January 18th to Sunday, January 28th Where Park City, Utah What Beginning in 1978, the Sundance Film Festival has grown to become the largest independent film festival in the United States. Situated in the quaint, yet exclusive town of Park City, Utah, the annual event attracts cinema lovers from all over the world and showcases the best of independent film. The first weekend is jam-packed with industry parties attended by stars and film aficionados, sponsor-led celebrity events and world premieres.

The 2017 Festival showcased 119 feature-length and 68 short films, alongside new episodic work, panels and music. The Sundance Film Festival has introduced international audiences to numerous groundbreaking films during the past three decades including: “Manchester by the Sea,” “Boyhood,” “Brooklyn,” “Twenty Feet from Stardom,” “Little Miss Sunshine,” “Reservoir Dogs,” “An Inconvenient Truth,” “Precious,” among others.

For Sundance 2018, Quintessentially has access to ticket packages, parties, premieres and more.
