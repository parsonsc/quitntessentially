Who we are

Professional, vigorous in cost savings and proactively intelligent in our approach Contact Us

GLOBAL INTELLigence, localised support

Established since 1971, QCTM is a dynamic Business Travel Management Company and specialist corporate division of Quintessentially, the world’s leading Lifestyle, Concierge and Travel service.

Professional, vigorous in cost savings and proactively intelligent in our approach, we guarantee to exceed expectations when it comes to our high-touch, personalised service and full suite of worldwide corporate travel management solutions.

an introduction to quintessentially

Since its launch in 2000, Quintessentially has been providing Members, Clients and Businesses with award-winning lifestyle management, concierge and travel services, offering specialist advice, insider access and exclusive privileges. With six global hubs, located in LA, New York, London, Dubai, Hong Kong, Melbourne and more than 60 offices on the ground, Quintessentially has cultural roots in communities around the world and benefits from a network of invaluable connections.

With in-depth experience in anticipating, fulfilling and tailoring the needs of individuals and corporate clients, Quintessentially operates 24/7/365 to meet Members’ requests. From the practical to the aspirational, Quintessentially provides an expert, localised service as well as a holistic corporate concierge offering. With a portfolio of over 15 highly specialised services from Art to Gifts, Travel to Real Estate, Quintessentially’s prestigious service is supported by a dedicated team of 2,500 Lifestyle Managers and Specialists, who work in over 25 languages.
